Aid for the forgotten animals

We're a group of volunteers aiming to directly help the needful stray dogs of the city by rehabilitating them, and giving them all the love, care and dignity they need and deserve.

Our Mission -

  • Providing a safe haven for all the neglected, abused and abandoned animals.
  • Providing veterinary services to as many dogs as possible for the safety of them as well as the people around them.
  • Promoting adoption and fostering by educating the public about the pitiful conditions of most stray dogs.

How were your donations used?

Rescuing and rehabilitating all abandoned and abused dogs

Providing food for all stray and abandoned dogs

Providing first aid to all neglected animals

Vaccinating all dogs against rabies

Keeping animals comfy, especially in cold weather

Conducting sterilization camps to combat overpoulation

Global Aid Unlimited is a non profit and volunter based organization, ensuring that 100% of your proceeding goes to helping the needful.

Whatever amount you choose to contribute, feel assured that your generosity will be put to the best possible use to help the greatest number of animals.
