Aid for the forgotten animals

We're a group of volunteers aiming to directly help the needful stray dogs of the city by rehabilitating them, and giving them all the love, care and dignity they need and deserve. We wish to provide a safe haven for all the dogs who have nobody else to protect them.

Our Mission-

  • Providing a safe haven for all the neglected, abused and abandoned animals
  • Providing veterinary services to as many dogs as possible for the safety pf them as well as the people around them.
  • Promoting adoption and fostering by educating the public about the pitiful conditions of most street dogs
